# **Chinese Orchestra**

Greenridge Secondary School and Fajar Secondary School will merge in January 2023.  
Chinese Orchestra will not be recruiting new members in 2023.

![](/images/CO1.jpg)

The Chinese Orchestra has performed at various events, which include the Lunar New Year Concert, the Music Exchange Programme at Mayflower Secondary School, the German European School (Singapore) Collaboration 2019 and a presentation during the schoolâ€™s morning assembly. In the 2021 Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation, the Chinese Orchestra was awarded a Certificate of Distinction in recognition of its efforts and excellence. In addition, the Chinese Orchestra has also engaged the community by participating in the Creative Arts Carnival at Bukit Panjang Library and Choa Chu Kang Library as part of the Performing Arts Community Project, organised by the National Library Board.
