<h3><strong>Producing Stories That Spark Change</strong>&nbsp;</h3>
<p>In today’s media-rich world, stories can inform, challenge and inspire.
Through New Media Journalism, our students learn to use digital platforms
to discuss real issues, highlight multiple perspectives and advocate for
meaningful causes.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students create social media-style short-form videos, video and audio
podcasts, and feature video documentaries. Whether they are behind the
camera, in front of the lens or behind the microphone, they learn to research,
interview, script, present and persuade with confidence.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>Photo 12: Conversations that connect, exploring youth perspectives, one mic at a time</em>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Guided by media professionals and authentic learning experiences, our
students develop critical thinking by asking whose voices are heard, whose
perspectives are missing and how their stories can move others to care.
Through showcases, they grow as thoughtful storytellers who use media to
spark conversations and champion change.&nbsp;</p>
