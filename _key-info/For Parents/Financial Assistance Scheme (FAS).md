<h1>Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)</h1>
<p>The Ministry of Education provides financial assistance to needy Singapore
Citizen students in Government or Government-aided schools so that all
Singaporeans, regardless of their financial background, can benefit from
the best opportunities in education. Eligibility and benefits of MOE Financial
Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS) is available at the official MOE website at
the following link: https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance
You may also download the information sheet here: <a href="/files/FAS/MOE_FAS_2026_Info_Sheet.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE FAS 2026 Info Sheet </a>.</p>
<h5><u>How to Apply</u></h5>
<p>You are strongly encouraged to apply for MOE FAS via the online e-FAS
application which is also available on Parents Gateway (under Services
tab – Financial Assistance) via Singpass login.</p>
