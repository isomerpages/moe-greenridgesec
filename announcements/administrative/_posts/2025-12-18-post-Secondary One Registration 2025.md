<h3><strong>Secondary 1 Registration 2025&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Welcome to Greenridge Secondary School!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that incoming Secondary 1 students are <strong>NOT REQUIRED</strong> to
physically report to school upon receiving their S1 Posting results on
19 December 2025.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The school will be utilising Parents Gateway (PG) as the main means of
communication with parents. Parents/Guardians of incoming Secondary 1 students
will receive information via PG by our Secondary 1 Year Head, Mr Wong Shan
Jing on 20 December 2025.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents who have not downloaded the PG app can refer to the link below
for more information: <a href="https://pg.moe.edu.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">pg.moe.edu.sg</a>&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please call our General Office at 67691491 if you have any further questions.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Click <a href="/files/Secondary One 2026/Information_Kit_for_2026_Secondary_1_Students_and_Parents.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>here</u></a> for the “<em>Information Kit for 2026 Secondary 1 Students and Parents</em>”.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong><u>Appeal for Admission to Greenridge Secondary School</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please note that only students whose PSLE score meets the school’s cut-off
point will be considered.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Admission via appeal is subject to the availability of vacancies and the
school's assessment of the applicant's suitability.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/Guardians who wish to transfer your child/ward into Greenridge
Secondary School due to <strong>non-medical conditions</strong>, please
click on the following link to complete the Appeal Form: <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/gsssec1appeal2025</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All appeals are to be submitted by <strong>26 December 2025, 12.00pm</strong>. <strong>Late submission will not be allowed.</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students should still report to their posted secondary school on 2 January
2026.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The application is deemed to be unsuccessful if you do not hear from the
school by 2 January 2026.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>Transfer Out of Greenridge Secondary School</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Except in cases involving extenuating circumstances, for which parents/guardians may contact the posted secondary school for further advice</strong>,
all other parents/guardians who wish to transfer their child/ward to another
school will need to approach the school you wish to transfer to for the
admission procedures. If the child/ward is accepted by another school,
parents/guardians must submit the other school’s acceptance form to our
school for endorsement and the release of the child/ward.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
